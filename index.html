Beyoncentration

An aesthetic history of Bey, aka YONCE, Sasha Fierce, Mrs. Carter, and Third Ward Trill (nee Beyoncé Giselle Knowles), and her artistic evolution through the years. Y'all know how to play: click two boxes to uncover the images underneath. If it's a match, they both stay visible. If not, they turn back over. The game ends when all the pairs have been uncovered. To make the game more challenging, add a row and then press reset.
